CUPS WON BY AUSTRALIAN MEN'S CRICKET

FIRST WORLD CUP:

The 1987 Cricket world cup was jointly hosted by India and Pakistan.
Australia emerged victorious by defeating England in the final and won its maiden ICC world cup trophy.
Australian Cricket team was lead by Allen Border.


SECOND WORLD CUP:

Australia won the second world cup in the year 1999 by defeating Pakistan in the finals.
The tournament was hosted by England.

THIRD WORLD CUP:

Australia's third world cup win was in the year 2003.
The tournament was hosted by South Africa,Kenya and Zimbabwe.
Australia won the third world cup by defeating India in the finals.
The captain who lead Australia for its third world cup was Ricky Ponting.


FOURTH WORLD CUP:

Australia won its fourth world cup in the year 2007 and became the first team to win 3 consecutive cups.
Australia defeated Sri Lanka in the finals to win its fouth cup.
The Australian side was lead by Ricky Ponting for the second time.
The tournament was hosted by West Indies.


FIFTH WORLD CUP:

Australia won the fifth world cup in the year 2015.
Australia became the team that has won most number of world cups.
The Australian side was lead by Michael Clarke.
The tournament was host by Australia and New Zealand jointly.


SIXTH WORLD CUP:

Australia won their sixth world cup in the year 2023, the same year after their test championship win This time also they deafeated India in the finals at their home.
The Australian side was lead by Pat Cummins.

FIRST T20 WORLD CUP:

Australia won its first T20I world cup in the year 2021.
The Australian side was lead by Aaron Finch
Australia deafeated New Zealand and won its maiden T20I worldcup.
The tournament was hosted by Oman and UAE jointly,it was scheduled to take place in India but was changed due to COVID-19.


FIRST TEST CHAMPIONSHIP:

Australia won the second season of world test championship by defeating Indian in the finals that took place in England.
The Australian side was lead by Pat Cummins.

With this victory Australia became the only team to win a cup in all formats
